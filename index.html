SBIPS

Sunnybrook Brain Image Processing Software

This software library was developed by the Linda C. Campbell Cognitive Neurology Research Group at Sunnybrook Research Institute under the direction of Dr. Sandra Black.
PROJECTS

itksnapsb
A modified version of itk-SNAP for viewing, editing and landmarking medical images.

sips
A software package designed primarily for automated tissue and lesion segmentation as well as the semi-automated parcellation of brain regions.
